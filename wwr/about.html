White Water Rafting

White water rafting, also known as whitewater kayaking or simply rafting, is an outdoor recreational activity that involves navigating a river or other bodies of fast-moving water in an inflatable raft or kayak. The goal of white water rafting is to traverse the water's turbulent and challenging sections, which are often characterized by rapids, rocks, and other obstacles.
