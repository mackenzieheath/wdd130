Overview

Purpose

Throught an informative website and active social media profiles, we aim to showcase our array of exhilarating rafting trips, highlight our commitment to safety and environmental sustainability, and engage with our customers directly. This online presence will not only streamline the booking process but also enable us to share captivating stories, photos, and videos of our rafting experiences, fostering a sense of community among our loyal patrons and newcomers alike. Ultimately, our online presence will enhance our visibility, customer engagement, and overall business growth in the competative world of water rafting companies.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website comprises adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers seeking thrilling experiences in natural settings. Who are the target customers? Our target customers are individuals aged 18 to 45, both singles and families, with a moderate to comfortable financial situation. They have a strong interest in outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, and water sports. They value experiences over material possessions and prioritize spending their leisure time in nature. What do they want? Our target customers are seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures in the great outdoors. They want to escape the daily grind, connect with nature, and create lasting memories. They desire well-organized, safe, and professionally guided water rafting trips that cater to various skill levels, from beginners to experienced rafters. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? When accessing our website, our target audience expects to find comprehensive information about our rafting trips, including trip durations, difficulty levels, equipment provided, and pricing. They also want to see photos and videos showcasing the breathtaking landscapes and thrilling rapids they will encounter on our adventures. Additionally, they seek clear instructions on how to make reservations, details on safety measures, and testimonials from past customers to assure them of a memorable and secure experience.
